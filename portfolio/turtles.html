Turtles!

It felt like all my close friends were spawning, so I set a goal of making toys that would make good baby gifts. I naively thought this would be a quick project...Turns out the dimensions are sensitive enough I had to break down and buy plans after many, many prototypes. I adjust the purchased plans to my liking and then built 10. I think they are remarkably cute and satisfying. Finished with mineral oil which is safe for babies to put in their mouths, but meant they took a good 2 weeks to dry.
