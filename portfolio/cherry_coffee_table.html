Cherry Coffee Table

I'm a bit salty about this piece. I built this as a wedding present for two very good friends. AND THEY DIDN'T HAVE A WEDDING. Blah, blah, blah, something about covid, blah, blah, blah. Moral of the story is don't do things ahead of time.

I am quite pleased with how it turned out. The large compound-angled legs were a fun problem to figure out. The top, shelf, and legs were butt glued together before coming back with a router to create the holes for the floating tenons. Table still resides at the home of my married (but no wedding) friends.

Size: 50” long x 32” deep x 20” tall
